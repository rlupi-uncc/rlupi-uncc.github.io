Neighborhood Cleanup — First Saturday

Recurs: First Saturday of every month Next meetup: Calculating… Meet: Preston Forest Neighborhood Park

Keep Preston Forest beautiful! Join neighbors for a friendly litter pick-up and light grounds tidying. Bring gloves if you have them; bags provided. Family-friendly.

What to bring

Comfortable shoes, water bottle, work gloves (optional), hat/sunscreen.

Thanksgiving Food Drive

Date: Saturday, Nov 15, 2025 Location: Preston Forest Park (main pavilion)

Help us support local families this season. We’re collecting non-perishable items (canned vegetables, rice, pasta, shelf-stable milk, peanut butter, etc.). Drop-offs welcome throughout the event window at the main pavilion.

Suggested donation list

Canned proteins, canned vegetables/fruit, pasta/rice, cereal/oatmeal, shelf-stable milk, baby formula.
