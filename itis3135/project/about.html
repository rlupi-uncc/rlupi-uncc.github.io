About the Preston Forest Neighborhood Association

The Preston Forest Neighborhood Association (PFNA) is a volunteer-run organization dedicated to enhancing the quality of life within our community. We work together to maintain the safety, beauty, and neighborly spirit of Preston Forest through collaboration, communication, and local initiatives.

Information

Our Mission

To foster a sense of community among residents by maintaining shared spaces, promoting sustainability, and organizing events that bring neighbors together. PFNA supports safety, inclusion, and pride in our neighborhood.

Our History

Founded in the early 1980s, Preston Forest grew into a close-knit neighborhood with residents who value connection and care for the environment. The association began when homeowners saw the need for coordination in maintaining common areas and communicating with city partners. Over the years, PFNA has led numerous beautification projects and community gatherings that keep the spirit of Preston Forest alive.

Our Goals
  • Encourage community participation in neighborhood cleanups and projects
  • Maintain communication with city officials about local issues and safety
  • Promote sustainable landscaping and recycling initiatives
  • Organize family-friendly events and seasonal celebrations
  • Preserve and enhance the natural environment of our parks and trails
Community Initiatives
  • Neighborhood Watch: A resident-led safety awareness program working with local law enforcement.
  • Green Spaces Program: Volunteers dedicated to planting trees and maintaining public areas.
  • Welcome Committee: A small group that greets new neighbors and introduces them to PFNA activities.
  • Annual Yard of the Month: Recognition for homeowners who demonstrate pride in their property upkeep.
