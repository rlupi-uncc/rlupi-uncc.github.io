Project Overview

The Maplewood Neighborhood Association Website will serve as an informational hub for residents and visitors. It will promote awareness of community initiatives, highlight upcoming events, and showcase neighborhood projects through engaging, read-only interactivity such as slideshows, galleries, and expandable content.

Intended Users

  • Neighborhood residents seeking news and updates
  • New homeowners learning about the community
  • Local organizations and partners supporting neighborhood efforts

Overview of Content

  • Home page with mission and latest updates
  • About page with history and leadership information
  • Events page listing upcoming and past activities
  • Gallery page showcasing photos of projects and gatherings
  • Map page highlighting local landmarks and resources

Page Design

Home (index.html)

  • Purpose: Introduce the neighborhood and display the latest community updates.
  • Audience: Residents and public visitors.
  • Content: Mission statement, slideshow banner, brief news blurbs.
  • Interactivity: JavaScript slideshow and “read more” content toggles.
  • Actions: Navigation to About, Events, Gallery, and Map pages.

About (about.html)

  • Purpose: Provide background, mission, and leadership details of the association.
  • Audience: Current and prospective members.
  • Content: History section, mission statement, board member list with photos.
  • Interactivity: jQuery accordion for sections; hover tooltips on board photos.

Events (events.html)

  • Purpose: Display upcoming and past community events.
  • Audience: Neighborhood residents and local supporters.
  • Content: Event listings with dates and summaries.
  • Interactivity: Event filter buttons (Upcoming/Past) and collapsible details.
  • Actions: Links to related photos in Gallery.

Gallery (gallery.html)

  • Purpose: Showcase photos from neighborhood activities and beautification projects.
  • Audience: Residents, media, and potential sponsors.
  • Content: Categorized image gallery with captions.
  • Interactivity: jQuery Lightbox and AJAX “load more” feature (read-only).

Map (map.html)

  • Purpose: Display a map of the neighborhood and local resources.
  • Audience: Residents and newcomers to the community.
  • Content: Embedded Google Map, meeting locations, safety and utility info.
  • Interactivity: Expandable sections for resource categories; clickable map markers.

Dynamic Functionality

While the site is informational only, several read-only interactive elements will enhance usability and engagement:

  • Home: JavaScript slideshow for banner images and toggleable “read more” sections.
  • About: Accordion content panels and hover tooltips on board photos.
  • Events: Filterable event listings using jQuery buttons and dynamic content reveal.
  • Gallery: Lightbox photo viewer with AJAX-based image loading (read-only JSON source).
  • Map: Expandable lists for local resources and interactive Google Maps embed.
